How To Join The Game

  1. Login to Roblox (If not already)
  2. Join "Emergency Response: Liberty county"
  3. In the top right corner next to your money you should see a MENU icon. Click on the MENU icon
  4. Once in the MENU click on "Servers" then click on "Join By Code"
  5. Enter "BlackCity", then click "Join"
