BCRP IS COOMING SOON!

Black City Roleplay is a fun community on Roblox. We are a well organised discord server. Our staff will try their best to help you in any way they can.
They also read all suggestions and tickets made on the Discord.
We strive for excellence, and professionalism. We were once the biggest server in ER:LC, having about well over 2300 members, however the older server was shut down due to some complications.
Black City was restarted by 3GHXXT in late 2021, and had 700 members before shutting down and restarting in November of 2021, and now, we are here.
We have well over 1000 members in just a few weeks time. Everyone in the staff team is grateful and we are looking to grow further.


Our Goal

Our goal for the Server and the Discord is to gain as many members as possible, while the professionality is at its finest.
