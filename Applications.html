Applications

Applications for Black City Roleplay are usually always open, but sometimes can have restrictions, for example "Only accepting EST".
When looking to apply always double check if the applications are open and if you meet the restrictions (If any).
When submitting your application please make sure they follow these rules:

  • Do not make a troll application
  • Do not go under the # sentence minimum
  • Do not enter false information

Any application that breaks these rules will most likely be declined, its just a waste of your time and the staff teams time.
